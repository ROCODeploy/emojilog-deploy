Shortcuts

AccessKey refers to a one or more shortcut keys followed by a single character. Usually it's Alt on Windows or Control+Alt on macOS, but it changes based on your browser and operating system.

Launcher refers to the app's command runner: press Alt+Enter, type the command, then press Enter to run.

Add to Home screen on mobile and tablet devices

This web app can be 'installed' and used as if it were a native mobile app (with an icon, working without internet access, running as a standalone app outside of the browser).

  1. Open the app in your browser, then follow the steps based on your operating system:

iOS + Safari

  1. Tap the Share button
  2. Tap Add to Home Screen

Android + Chrome

  1. Tap the More button
  2. Tap Add to home screen

What is remoteStorage?

remoteStorage is an incredible open-source technology for synchronizing data between multiple devices and making it available offline.

It allows the same data to be used in different ways by multiple apps. You could think of it as a USB key for your documents that you can plug into websites to work on your stuff.

You can get one for free from 5apps or host your own.
